Mohamed Ragab received the B.Sc. degree (First Class Hons.) in communication and electronics engineering and M.Sc. degree in medical image processing from Aswan University, Egypt, in 2014 and 2017, respectively.  He is currently working toward the Ph.D.  degree in computer science and engineering under the supervision of  [Dr. Kwoh Chee Keong](https://personal.ntu.edu.sg/asckkwoh/), Nanyang Technological University, Singapore.  He is jointly working with the Machine Intellection Department (MI),  [Institute of Infocomm Research (I2R)](https://www.a-star.edu.sg/i2r), A*STAR  under the supervision of [Dr. Zhenghua Chen](https://zhenghuantu.github.io/) and [ Prof. Li Xiaoli](https://personal.ntu.edu.sg/xlli/).  Recently, He joined as Sciesntist at both Machine Intelleiction Department, I2R and Center for Fronetiers AI Research (CFAR).
